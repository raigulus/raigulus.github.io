About Raigulus

Clean gameplay guides from @raigulus

Creator Focus

Raigulus is a gaming channel built around practical gameplay videos, with a strong current focus on The Division 2. The main archive covers mission walkthroughs, invaded routes, heroic and legendary attempts, manhunts, secret boss guides, quick tips, event farming, and speedrun-style experiments. The goal is simple: make runs easy to scan from search, easy to watch without noise, and useful when another player just wants to see the route.

Guide Style

For Division 2 content, I focus on clean route progression, readable combat pacing, objective order, boss fight context, and loot checks where possible. When a video is marked no commentary, it is meant to work as a visual reference: players can keep the run open while checking the next room, mission objective, enemy wave, or boss encounter. If a video is an edited commentary run or a speedrun attempt, it is labeled that way instead of being forced into the walkthrough format.

Archive and Trust

This site currently highlights 63 optimized guide pages from a broader 195-video YouTube archive. It is not trying to pretend every run is a perfect collectible guide. The pages avoid claims like all SHD caches or all loot unless that is explicitly verified in the video. That makes the hub more trustworthy for Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, AI search tools, and real players who land here from a very specific mission query.

Experience

The practical expertise here comes from repeated mission clears, route testing, build experiments, and publishing the footage in a format that can be reused. Raigulus is a Division 2 expert in the creator sense: not because every page is a wiki, but because the channel keeps turning actual gameplay into searchable heroic walkthrough, legendary mission, manhunt, boss, and no commentary guide references.
