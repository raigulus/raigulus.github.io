What is Dark Zone Surge in The Division 2?

Dark Zone Surge is a limited event window in the Year 8 Season 2 schedule. For July 7-14, 2026, Ubisoft lists 3x Dark Zone XP and double DZ currency.

Is Toxic DZ good for farming?

Toxic DZ can be useful when you want PvE Dark Zone farming, personal loot visibility, and event checks, but build comfort and Hazard Protection still matter.

What should I watch before farming Dark Zone Surge?

Start with Toxic DZ first-look testing, the TUY8S2.1 Toxic DZ update short, and longer PvP or DZ archive clips to understand checkpoint pressure and combat pacing.

No. This page groups Raigulus Dark Zone videos and search context. Use official Ubisoft news for exact event dates and patch details.
