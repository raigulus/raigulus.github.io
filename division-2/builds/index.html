FAQ

Are these the best The Division 2 builds?
No. This is a video-backed archive of real build tests, not a universal tier list. Each run has its own activity, difficulty, event state, player group, and patch context.
Which builds are tested here?
The current archive includes Bluescreen plus Tipping Scales Tier 10 tests, a Whiplash LMG loop, a Liberty Perfection PvP match, Regulus farming footage, archived Rage Harvest runs, and Toxic Dark Zone build experiments.
Do these pages list exact gear and damage numbers?
Only when the video visibly supports them. Raigulus does not invent a full gear list, guaranteed damage number, or best-build claim from incomplete footage.
Will a build work in every activity?
Not necessarily. Difficulty, directives, group buffs, event stacks, expertise, enemy type, and player comfort can all change how a setup feels.
