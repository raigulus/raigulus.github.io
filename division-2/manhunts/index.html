What are manhunt walkthroughs?
These pages group Raigulus manhunt target routes, Scout objectives, and seasonal objective videos for The Division 2.
Are Scout week pages kept separate?
Yes. Scout pages are labeled with season and week context where known so Google, Bing, and AI tools can identify the objective set.
Can I use these as exact objective references?
Use the embedded videos and chapters as route references. Live seasonal requirements can change, so confirm the objective text in-game.
